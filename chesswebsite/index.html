About Our Knights Club

Welcome to the Knights Club, the official chess club affiliated with IIITS Sports. We have a rich history of achievements, strong connections, and a dedicated group of skilled players. The club was founded on October 1, 2024. Our mission is to inspire and develop players who are passionate about chess.
